👕 **Wardrobe Management**

I help you organize your wardrobe and find the perfect outfit!

**📦 Available Commands:**
• /wardrobe - Open main menu

**💬 Natural Language:**
• "Show my wardrobe"

**🎛️ Menu Actions:**
• **Add Item** - Register new clothing piece
• **Remove Item** - Delete unwanted items
• **Show All** - Browse complete wardrobe

**✨ What I can do:**
• Categorize clothing by type (tops, pants, jackets, shoes, etc.)
• Suggest outfit combinations
• Provide weather-appropriate recommendations
