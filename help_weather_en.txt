🌦 Weather assistant

I'll help you find out the weather for today or the next few days - including temperatures, conditions and useful additional information!

💬 Natural language:
- “What will the weather be like tomorrow in Berlin?”
- “Weather in Hamburg on Thursday?”
- “What will the weather be like in Mögglingen in 2 days?”

🎛️ Features:
- Display weather - call up daily or 3-day forecast
- Recognize location - automatically read the city from your message
- Voice output - Have the weather report read aloud
- Display weather graphic - Visual preview of the weather situation at the sent location
